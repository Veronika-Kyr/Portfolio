Deruny app

From this page you can get general information about one of the most popular traditional Ukrainian food - Deruny . It also contains the recipe of this dish. To get deeper into the atmosphere of Ukraine, design of this page includes traditional Ukrainian colors and ornament, allows to read the recipe in Ukrainian language

Lord of Weather app
